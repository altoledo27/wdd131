SprachSchatz | Learning German Site

Site Name: SprachSchatz

Meaning "language treasure" in German, suggesting valuable language resources.

Purpose of the Site

The site will collect useful resources for language learning, share a learning plan based on the user's German level, and share some interesting facts about the language.

Scenarios

  • "I've never studied German before. Where should I start?"
  • "What simple German books or stories can I read as a beginner?"
  • "Are there any German podcasts suitable for intermediate learners?"
